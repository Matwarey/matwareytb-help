Welcome to Matwarey Utility!

Matwarey Utility is a modification for Minecraft: PE which improves your gaming experience.

Mouse support

Matwarey Utility supports mouse & keyboard play on Android 8 and above.

Resource Pack skip

You can skip forced resource packs when joining servers with Matwarey Utility.

Flying

Enables flying even in survival.

No-clip

Phase/ghost through blocks. (NOTE: can take damage)

High Jump

Once toggled, it gives you a slider to adjust the jump height. (NOTE: can take damage)

Speed

Once toggled, it gives you a slider to adjust the movement speed (flying/walking/running).

Auto-Sprint

Automatically sprint when moving.

No Bow Slowdown

Aiming with a bow won't slow you down.

Slow Falling

Slows your fall to prevent some fall damage similar to the feather falling enchant

Blink

Sends pings to your internet also preventing a total shut off to your internet whilst at the same time allowing you to still be connected. Elytra Fly

This fixes flying on some servers by putting on the elytra which also enables you to use farpick (reach) to its full power.

Tap Teleport

Click where you want/can to tap and it will teleport you to that spot.

Bridge Builder

Automatically builds a bridge for you as you're walking using the block in-hand.

Chest Stealer

Open a chest and it'll automatically take the items.

Nuke

Once toggled, it gives you a slider to adjust the size of the nuker. It makes big holes; destroy a block and it'll nuke the area.

Force Achievements

Allows you to earn achievements even in a world with cheats enabled..

Rapid Build

There are two ways to use Rapid Build; command mode or without command mode. It lets you select an area and will build/copy/paste for you automatically. Command mode requires you to have cheats enabled and be the server operator.

Give item

Opens up a menu with every item even unobtainable items and adds the item to your inventory.

Enchant

Opens up the enchantment menu in which enchants the item held in your hand.

Spawn Experience Orb

Automatically spawns experience orbs for you to gain XP.

Reach

Once toggled, it gives you a slider in which increases the range of reach to pick/destroy/place blocks.

Reach Fix (Server)

Bypasses the servers range restriction enabling you to use it.

Name Override

Lets you spoof/change your in-game name.

X-Ray

Once toggled, it gives you the option of which ores you wish to see underground.

ChestESP

Puts a colored outlined square around nearby chests for easy finding.

PlayerESP

Puts a colored outlined box around nearby players to see them through walls.

Block Tracker

Detects block changes.

Entity Outlines

Adds outlines around nearby players and mobs to see them through walls.

Free-cam

Lets you move a camera around without moving your actual player. (NOTE: will not take damage, can not /open or interact with anything. good for cave finding.)

Fullbright

Makes every block bright to the max and visible.

Armor Hud

Displays your armors health.

HP Bars

Displays health points on all entities head (players/mobs).

Minimap

Once toggled, minimap will show on screen and provides options to set it up.

Zoom

On the slider, click and drag to adjust the view of how far you want to see.

ADD

Type preset commands like /gamemode, preset responses for chat, and/or spam chat. (NOTE: only works when you have the Operator permission and with, cheats enabled)

Kill-Aura

Once toggled, it will give you options to choose who are marked (mob/player). It automatically attacks mobs and players within your radius.

Anti-Knockback

Prevent players or mobs from knocking you backwards.

Auto-Bow

Bow aimbot, when you go to fire it snaps to entities.

Teleport to Player

Once toggled, it will open up a dialogue in which will show you the nearest player(s) within your range and once you click their name, it will teleport you to them.

Hitbox Expand

Pops up the hitbox multiplier slider to increase the size of the entitys hitbox.
